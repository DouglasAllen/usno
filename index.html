USNO

The United States Naval Observatory provides a number of public facing data services. Those that this gem supports will be listed here for reference.

usno-imagery
provides synthetic views of major solar system objects from various perspectives
usno-transit
provides access to forms A & B, returning rise, set, and transit data for major solar system objects and bright stars for cities in the US & locations world-wide.

Installation

Add this line to your application's Gemfile:

gem 'usno'

And then execute:

$ bundle

Or install it yourself as:

$ gem install usno

Usage

Require the library/libaries you wish to use:

require usno/imagery # For synthetic images of Earth and selected solar system bodies.
require usno/transit # For rise/set/transit times for major solar system bodies and selected bright stars
